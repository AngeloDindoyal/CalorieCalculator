This calculator determines the amount of calories that you have to consume to maintain your currrent weight. For the activity level, exercise/light exercise is considered as 15-30 minutes of elevated heart rate activity, and intense exercise is considered as 45-120 minutes of elevated heart rate activity. The accuracy of the equations used have been proven; however, there are still other factors that affect total daily expenditure that are not considered. Use the results of this calculator as a general idea of your own daily energy expenditure to find the exact number.
